# Aerobott
Aerobott — Official Company Website

The complete digital presence of Aerobott — a drone technology company. Covers company identity, services, training, pilot hiring, and a CMS-powered blog. Live at aerobott.com

🌐 Live Site
https://aerobott.com


📋 About
Aerobott is a drone technology company operating across India. This is the company's official website and primary digital identity — it introduces the company, its vision and team, and serves as the central hub for all its offerings: professional drone services, pilot training programs, client bookings, and an industry blog.
Built and deployed as a production full-stack application, the site handles real client traffic on aerobott.com.

✨ Features

🏠 Landing Page — Animated hero section with company overview and CTAs
🛰️ Services — Detailed breakdown of drone service offerings (inspection, survey, photography, etc.)
🎓 Drone Training — Course listings and training program information
📋 Hire a Drone — Service booking and pilot hiring flow for clients
📰 Blog — CMS-powered blog with full content management via Wagtail admin
👥 About / Team — Company story and team profiles
📬 Contact — Lead capture and inquiry form
